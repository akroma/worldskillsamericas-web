WorldSkills Americas Bogotá 2014

WorldSkills Americas 2014 is an international vocational training competition to be held in Bogota, Colombia, on the 1st of April to the 4th, 2014. This app will allow you to better enjoy the event, armed with quick ways of understanding each skill, browsing which countries are competing for the gold medal, and what events will be happening througout the entire competition week.

Features

Skills Information

Interested in the skill in front of you? Curious about what the competitors are doing? Which countries are participating? The app puts all this information at your fingertips, allowing you to quickly understand the competitor's goals in their work, and a bit of the history behind each skill.

Up-to-date news and events

Stay aware of the competition's schedule, receiving realtime updates of upcoming events and news articles on your device.

Venue map

Confused by all these skills? Looking for an specific one? The app provides you with a full map of the competition's venue, along with the location of all relevant spots, from the skills to washrooms to infirmary wards.

About Us

WorldSkills Americas (WSA) is a non-governmental organization, not for profit, and has as main objective to provide means for vocational education quality improvement, by the means of exchange between North America, Central America and Caribbean and South America countries, involving governments, industries, commerce and vocational education and training organizations/institutions.

WorldSkills Americas with cooperation of its member countries, every two years organize a competition focused on vocational training in one of its member countries. The first competition was held in 2010, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

WorldSkills Americas operates in conjunction with WorldSkills International.
